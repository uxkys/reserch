Icon Click Game User Manual

Game Overview

Game Name: Shape Puzzle Game (Icon Click Game)

URL: https://uxkys.github.io/reserch/iconclick

Objective: Identify the shape that appears most frequently among randomly placed shapes and click the corresponding icon button at the bottom to advance to the next level.

Session Duration: 3 minutes

Total Levels: 200

How to Play

Starting the Game

  1. Open index.html in your browser. Click the link on that page to navigate to the Icon Click Game page.
  2. On the game page, click the Start button displayed at the center. This will start a 3-minute session beginning at Level 1.

Gameplay

  • Each level consists of 12 shapes randomly placed on the screen without overlapping.
  • One shape type appears most frequently (this is the correct answer for that level).
  • Clicking a shape will change it (e.g., ▲ becomes △, ■ becomes □, ● becomes ○, ★ becomes ☆) and increase the click counter displayed under the corresponding button. A shape, once clicked, cannot be clicked again.
  • At the bottom, there are four icon buttons in the following order: ●, ▲, ■, ★. Click the button corresponding to the correct shape to clear the level and move on.
  • If an incorrect button is clicked, an alert ("Incorrect!") will be shown, and you must try again.

Level Progression & Difficulty

  • Levels progress automatically from Level 1 to Level 200 as you clear each level.
  • The difficulty increases over levels by reducing the number of the correct shape. Specifically, Level 1 has 5 correct shapes, and Level 200 has 3 correct shapes, making it harder to determine which shape appears most frequently.

Session End & Data Download

  • The session ends when 3 minutes have elapsed or when you clear Level 200.
  • Upon session end, a "Download CSV" button appears at the bottom right. Clicking this button downloads a CSV file containing your play data (play count, session start/end times, and clear time for each level).

Additional Notes

  • Once a shape has been clicked and changed, it cannot be clicked again.
  • Quick decision-making is essential as the session is limited to 3 minutes.
  • Your performance data for each level is recorded, allowing you to review your playing trends.
